    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find quick answers to the most common questions about our services, appointments, doctors, insurance, and patient care.

    How can I book an appointment?

    You can schedule an appointment online through our website, call our reception, or visit the hospital directly.

    What are your consultation hours?

    Our outpatient departments are open Monday to Saturday, from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Emergency services are available 24/7.

    Do I need an appointment before visiting?

    Appointments are recommended to reduce waiting time, but walk-in patients are also welcome based on doctor availability.

    Which insurance plans do you accept?

    We accept most major health insurance providers. Please contact our support team to confirm your specific plan.

    Can I choose my preferred doctor?

    Yes. While booking your appointment, you can select your preferred doctor from the available specialists.

    How can I access my medical reports?

    Medical reports can be collected from the hospital or provided digitally, depending on the service and patient preference.
